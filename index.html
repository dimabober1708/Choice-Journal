Troubleshooting

  • Restart the app and ensure you are on the latest version.
  • Restart your device (simple, but often effective).
  • If syncing is enabled, confirm you are signed into iCloud and have a stable connection.
  • If an entry appears missing, check filters and search.
FAQ — Does Choice Journal give financial advice?

No. Choice Journal is a personal journaling and reflection tool. It helps you document decisions and outcomes, but it does not provide financial, investment, tax, or legal advice.

FAQ — How do refunds and subscriptions work?

Purchases made through the App Store are handled by Apple. For refunds or subscription management, please use your Apple ID settings or Apple’s official support channels.

FAQ — How do I report a bug?

Email us with: (1) what you expected to happen, (2) what happened instead, (3) steps to reproduce, and (4) screenshots if possible. Include your iOS version and device model.

Privacy Policy

Your trust matters. This policy explains what information Choice Journal may process, why, and what choices you have. If you have questions, contact us at dimabober1708@icloud.com.

In plain English

  • Your journal is for you. We don’t sell personal data.
  • We aim to collect the minimum needed to run and improve the app.
  • You can request help or deletion guidance by email.

Effective date

  • Last updated: December 24, 2025
  • Applies to: Choice Journal (iOS)

1. Information We Process

  • Journal content you create: entries, notes, tags, and reflections (the “Content”).
  • Support communications: when you email us, we receive your email address and the information you choose to include.
  • Optional diagnostics (only if enabled/used in the app): basic crash logs or performance data to improve stability. This is typically collected in an aggregated or pseudonymous form.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core functionality (creating, viewing, and managing your journal entries).
  • To respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues.
  • To improve app reliability and user experience (e.g., crash diagnostics), where applicable.

3. Data Storage & Sync (If Applicable)

  • Your Content may be stored locally on your device.
  • If you enable iCloud sync (or similar Apple services), your Content may be stored and synchronized through Apple’s infrastructure under your Apple ID settings.

4. Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We share information only when necessary to provide the service (for example, Apple services you choose to use) or to comply with legal obligations.
  • If optional crash/analytics tools are used, they receive limited technical data strictly to help diagnose issues and improve stability.

5. Security

  • We apply reasonable safeguards to protect information in transit and at rest where applicable.
  • No system is 100% secure; please use device protections such as a passcode/Face ID.

6. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can edit or delete entries within the app.
  • If you contact us, you may request assistance related to your data by email.
  • If syncing is enabled, you can manage iCloud settings in iO
